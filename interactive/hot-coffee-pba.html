The Mission

PBA Brief

Your fast-food company needs a coffee container. It must:

⚡ Cool quickly from 200°F down to 130°F
⌛ Stay between 110°F and 130°F as long as possible
Above 130°F — too hot (burn risk)
110°F – 130°F — drinkable zone
Below 110°F — too cold
